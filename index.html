About

A curiosity-first club for the cosmos.

The Astrophysics Club is a student-run club at Fremont High School. We meet every other Wednesday to work through real astrophysics: the physics behind stars, black holes, exoplanets, relativity and cosmology, from first principles to real data.

Everything below is drawn from the club's real record: thirteen meetings, sixteen handouts, one year that ran from the scale of the universe to the edges of time.
